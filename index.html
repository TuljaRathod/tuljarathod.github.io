Automation & Workflows

n8n Workflows Shipped

Both workflows were built to solve real operational pain points — eliminating manual effort, surfacing signal faster, and closing the loop between support data and product decisions.

🤖 RCA Bot — DevRev Support Tickets

Problem: Support tickets in DevRev lacked structured root cause data, making it hard to identify recurring issues and drive product fixes.

Solution: Built an n8n workflow that automatically processes incoming DevRev support tickets, runs AI-assisted root cause classification, and enriches tickets with structured RCA tags — feeding a feedback loop directly into product prioritisation.

Outcome: Reduced manual categorisation effort, surfaced patterns across WMS modules, and gave the product team a data trail from user complaint to root cause.

n8n DevRev API AI Classification RCA Ticket Automation

📢 Daily Support Update — Slack

Problem: The support team had no automated visibility into daily ticket status — stakeholders relied on manual check-ins and ad hoc Slack messages to understand what was open, blocked, or resolved.

Solution: Built an n8n workflow that runs on a daily schedule, queries ticket data, and posts a structured Slack update broken down by pod, stage, and owner — giving each team instant visibility into their queue without any manual effort.

Outcome: Eliminated daily stand-up prep time, improved accountability across pods, and created a persistent async record of support health.

n8n Slack API Scheduled Trigger Pod / Stage / Owner Async Reporting
